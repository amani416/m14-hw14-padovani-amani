Welcome to Amani's Page

I’m Amani Padovani — a multidisciplinary digital artist with a global perspective and a passion for bringing ideas to life through design, code, and storytelling.

Born in Sweden, I moved to the United States of America when I was 5 years old. Raised in Central Florida, I graduated from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Arts in Digital Arts and Sciences in 2017. I am currently back in school, pursuing a Master's certificate from the College of Journalism and Communications at my alma mater. My multicultural background—born to a Palestinian refugee mother and an Irish-American father—has given me a unique ability to connect with people from all walks of life and navigate diverse perspectives in both personal and professional settings.

I’m a multifaceted creative digital artist who blends creativity with tech. My skill set spans multiple disciplines, I’m comfortable working across design, code, video, animation, and even a bit of interactive game development. I’m especially handy with the Adobe Creative Cloud, web tools, and anything that brings ideas to life visually or digitally. I’m outgoing, easy to work with, and always up for learning something new. Whether it’s designing a logo, editing a video, building a website, or figuring out a new tool, I love the challenge and the creative process behind it.

After earning my BA in Digital Arts & Sciences from the University of Florida in 2017, I was promoted to Training & Development Lead at LEGOLAND Florida, where I led the launch of a new learning management system (LMS) that integrated training materials, certification testing, and interactive games.

I also cross-trained across departments, which gave me valuable insight into the operations of a manufacturing company.
